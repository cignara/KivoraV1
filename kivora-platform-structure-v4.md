# Kivora – Platform Structure

### Version 4.0 · Aligned with `Kivora_Master_Activity_Plan.md` (v4, corrected) · June 2026

### Single Launch · All 9 Worlds · Ages 2–11 · English Only · Worldwide Audience

> **Changelog from v3.0:**
> - `Markets` changed from "USA · UK" to **Worldwide**. The platform is positioned for children everywhere, not bounded to two markets.
> - `Curriculum Alignment` reframed: Common Core (USA), UK National Curriculum (KS1–KS2), EYFS, and NGSS Science remain as named reference frameworks (they're concrete, well-specified, and many international schools already benchmark against them), but are now presented as *examples within* a broader claim of alignment to internationally-relevant core skills — not as the boundary of who the platform serves.
> - Grade Explorer table: grade rows now show "Common Core / UK NC equivalent" framing rather than presenting CCSS/KS1/KS2/EYFS as the only applicable standards — added a general age/grade-band label per row so the table reads sensibly for a parent anywhere in the world.
> - Compliance items (COPPA, GDPR-K, UK GDPR, FERPA, Ofsted-ready reporting, WCAG 2.1) are retained unchanged — these are real regional regulations the platform supports, and a worldwide product can still offer region-specific compliance to the families and schools that need it.
> - Marketing copy ("for USA and UK families," "USA and UK perspectives," "kids across the USA and UK") generalized to worldwide framing, with USA/UK retained as examples where helpful.
>
> **Changelog from v2.0 (carried forward):**
> - Activity total corrected: 900 → **840** (sum of per-world Master Activity Plan counts). Special Needs' 145 items are a separate cross-world bucket.
> - Per-world activity counts corrected: Math Mountain 110→**130**, Story Cove 100→**80**, Creativity Kingdom 100→**65**, Brain Game Galaxy 90→**100**, Life Skills Village 85→**80**, Coding City 105→**65**.
> - Language: **English only**. Removed language selector, Sinhala/Tamil entries, bilingual nav links, footer language line, and `Languages` row (was "English" anyway — value unchanged but bilingual-mode references removed from pricing/dashboard feature lists).
> - Learning Paths reduced 26 → **24** (removed PATH-024 Sinhala, PATH-025 Tamil). Lesson Plans reduced 60 → **58** (removed LP-049, LP-050 bilingual lesson plans).
> - Sri Lankan-themed *content* (festival coloring pages, folk-tale stories) retained — these are content variety, not a language/locale feature.

> Embark on magical learning adventures with Kivi the Fox!
> A safe, gamified learning platform for children aged **2–11**.
